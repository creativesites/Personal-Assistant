# Zuri — Product Vision & Specification

> Zuri is an AI Relationship & Business Operating System. It continuously understands people, remembers what matters, reasons about every relationship, stays aware of the outside world, plans interactions, and executes communication and business operations — whether for your personal life, your business, or your team.

---

## 1. Core Philosophy

Zuri is not a messaging tool. It is not a CRM. It is an operating system for relationships and business execution.

The system expands intelligent relationship insights into complete operational execution:
- Features are capabilities of intelligent engines, not isolated tools
- The product is coherent whether managing 5 personal contacts, 5,000 customers, or an enterprise team
- Every interaction facilitated is intentional, goal-oriented, and backed by institutional memory
- The system improves continuously by learning from outcomes, conversations, and documents

---

## 2. Platform Modules & Engines

### Perception Layer
1. **Relationship Intelligence Engine** — Living psychological profiles, communication style, mood patterns, life context.
2. **Temporal Intelligence Engine** — Per-relationship clocks, cadence learning, dormancy alerts, morning coffee feed.
3. **Opportunity Detection Engine** — Buying signals, churn risks, birthdays, life events, follow-up gaps.
4. **World Knowledge Engine** — Real-time web search, market data, news-to-contact matching.

### Cognition Layer
5. **Conversation Strategy Engine** — Multi-turn conversation planning, objection handling, goal assignment.
6. **Knowledge Engine & AI Brain** — PDF/Excel/CSV document chunking, vector search, conversational Q&A, Knowledge Discovery Engine.
7. **Learning & Optimization Engine** — Model routing, tone tuning based on accepted/rejected suggestions.

### Execution Layer
8. **Autonomous Agent Engine** — Sales, Support, Community Manager agents with escalation rules and trust levels.
9. **Business Intelligence Platform** — Executive Health Score (0-100), 11 analytics sub-dashboards, ROI calculator, timeline.
10. **Business Workspace & ERP Engine** — Brand Kit, 15 Document types (Quotations, Invoices, Receipts, Purchase Orders, Contracts), Sales Orders, Bill of Materials, Inventory tracking, E-Signatures with 1:1 canvas pixel accuracy.
11. **Shared Team Inbox & Organization Governance** — Clerk Organizations integration, active conversation locking, collision warning indicators, workspace scoping.
12. **Career OS & CV Studio** — Scraped job workspace, readiness checklists, AI cover letters, resume studio.
13. **Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)** — Secure encryption for Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, and DashScope keys.
